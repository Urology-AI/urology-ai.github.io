# Clinical Tools

The lab builds and deploys research-to-clinic tools for surgeons, residents, and patients. Tools are validated on Mount Sinai cohorts before deployment.

## Prostate Cancer Risk Calculator

A pre-operative risk prediction tool covering post-RARP continence, potency, and margin status. Integrates clinical inputs (PSA, Gleason grade, clinical stage, nerve-sparing plan) with a validated machine learning model to generate individualized risk estimates.

*Status: Internal validation complete. External validation in progress.*

## Surgical Complexity Estimator

Estimates intra-operative complexity based on pre-op imaging features and patient anatomy, surfacing cases that may benefit from additional planning or senior surgeon involvement.

*Status: In development.*

## Operative Note Structuring Tool

LLM-assisted tool that extracts structured data from free-text operative notes — surgical phases, findings, complications, and technique variations — to support retrospective research and quality improvement.

*Status: Prototype.*
